🔧 Ignition Repair & Ignition Replacement

Ignition systems experience wear from daily use. If your car key will not turn, gets stuck in the ignition, or the vehicle refuses to start, the problem may not be the key — it may be the ignition system itself. Our mobile technicians come directly to your location with the tools needed to diagnose and fix ignition problems on-site.

⚙️ Common Ignition Problems

  • Key not turning in ignition
  • Key stuck in ignition
  • Ignition not turning at all
  • Broken key inside ignition
  • Ignition cylinder worn out
  • Vehicle not starting
  • Key difficult to insert or remove
  • Ignition switch not responding

🔍 Ignition Diagnosis

  • Ignition cylinder condition check
  • Key wear and damage assessment
  • Internal lock component inspection
  • Steering lock issue detection
  • Electrical ignition switch (if applicable)

🔧 Ignition Repair May Include

  • Cleaning and adjusting internal components
  • Repairing worn ignition pins
  • Fixing key alignment issues
  • Removing broken key pieces
  • Restoring smooth key rotation

🔄 Ignition Replacement Includes

  • Removing the damaged ignition cylinder
  • Installing a new ignition unit
  • Matching new ignition to your key
  • Programming (if required)
  • Testing full ignition function

🧠 Ignition Problems vs Key Problems

Signs it may be an ignition problem:

  • Key works in doors but not ignition
  • Key feels stuck or hard to turn
  • Ignition does not respond
  • Steering wheel lock interfering

Signs it may be a key issue:

  • Key worn or bent
  • Remote not working
  • Vehicle not detecting key
